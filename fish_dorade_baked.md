### Whole Baked Dorade  

#### Ingredients   
* 500 gram dorade  

#### Preparation    
1. Preheat oven to 200C  
2. Cut slices in side of dorade  
3. Coat with olive oil, salt, and pepper  
4. Stuff a few lemon slices in the cavity  


#### Directions   
1. Place in oven for 20 - 25 minutes
