## Cider Roasted Pork Tenderloin with Chutney   

#### Ingredients    
* Pork tenderloin
* Bottle of hard cider   
* 1/4 cup of maple syrup
* 3 tablespoons of salt
* 1 teaspoon of whole fennel seeds
* 1 teaspoon of pepper corns
* 1/2 teaspoon of whole corriander seeds
* 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
* Sliced ginger
* 1 1/2 tablespoons of chopped fresh rosemary

#### Preparation    
1. Make the marinade:
  a. Grind the spices
  b. Slice the ginger
  c. Chop the rosemary
  d. Put pork, ginger, rosemary, and marinade in a bag    
2. Marinade the pork overnight   
  
#### Directions   
1. Dry off the pork and lightly drizzle with olive oil, salt and pepper.    
2. Roast for 20 - 25 minutes at 450F.
3. Tent with foil and let rest for 10 minutes.
4. Serve with (fig) chutney.
