### Grilled Gambas on Sea Salt  

#### Ingredients  
* Gambas  
* Olive oil  
* Marinade: garlic, thyme  
* Sea salt  
* Lemon  

#### Preparation  

1. Trim and marinate the gambas for a few hours  
2. Cut lemon wedges  

#### Directions  

1. Put gambas on a bed of sea salt  

2. Grill under broiler for 1 - 2 minutes per side  

3. Season with lemon
