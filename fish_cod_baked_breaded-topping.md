## Ina Garten's Baked Cod with Breaded Topping    

#### Ingredients    
* Cod
* 1/2 cup of Ritz crackers   
* 1/2 cup of Japanese Panko   
* A bit of white wine
* Lemon
* 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh parsley
* 2 tablespoons of melted butter
* 2 garlic cloves, minced
* White wine   

#### Preparation   
1. Preheat oven to 400F
2. Mince garlic
3. Chop parsley
4. Melt butter
5. Make topping
  * Grind crackers in mixer and combine with Panko
  * Add chopped parsley and minced garlic
  * Add zest from the lemon    
  * Mix in melted butter to bind 
    
#### Directions   
1. Put a little olive oil, salt, pepper (and perhaps piment d'Esplette) on the fish in a baking pan.
2. Put in the oven for 10 minutes.
3. Remove from oven and pour a little white wine and lemon juice on the cod.
4. Cover with topping.
5. Return to the over for 12 minutes.
