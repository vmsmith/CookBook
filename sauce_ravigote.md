### Ravigote Sauce   

#### Ingredients    
* Red onion, chopped finely
* Capers
* Herbs (tarragon), chopped      
* 2 parts olive oil
* 1 part vinegar
* Salt, dash   
* Pepper, dash  

#### Preparation   
1. Chop onion
2. Chop herbs   

#### Directions   
1. Mix it, but don't emulsify it
