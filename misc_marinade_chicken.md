## Chicken Marinades   

### Basic

#### Ingredients    
* 1 clove garlic
* 1 shallot
* Zest of 1 lemons
* 6 tbls of lemon juice
* 1/2 tsp of paprika
* 1 tbls dried oregano
* 6 sprigs of fresh thyme
* 1/2 cup of olive oil
* 2 tsp of salt
* Pepper    

#### Preparation    
1. Grate the garlic
2. Grate the shallot
3. Zest the lemon
4. Chop the thyme   

#### Directions    
1. Mix it all
2. Marinade chicken in plastic bag 2 - 24 hours
