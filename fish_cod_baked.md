### Baked Cod  

#### Ingredients   
* Cod  
* Olive oil  
* Lemon(s)  
* Salt and pepper  
* Piment d'Eplette  

#### Preparation   

1. Juice the lemon  
2. Preheat oven to 400F/210C  

#### Directions  

1. Rub cod with oil and put in baking pan  
2. Add a little lemon juice  
3. Season with salt, pepper, and piment d'Eplette  
4. Bake for 10 - 12 minutes, depending on the thickness
