### Baked Rabbit à la Moutarde  

#### Ingredients  

* Rabbit legs    
* 1 large shallot, thinly sliced    
* Dijon mustard    
* Crème fraîche   
* Dry white wine   
* Butter  
* Salt and pepper      

#### Preparation   
1. Slice the shallot
2. Preheat oven to 170C     

#### Directions  
1. Salt and pepper the rabbit pieces well and set aside at room temperature for an hour.
2. Rub the baking dish with butter.
3. Lay the sliced shallots on the bottom of the baking dish.
4. Put the rabbit piece in, and "paint" a thin layer of mustard on them.
5. Put a nice layer of crème fraiche over the mustard.
6. Lay some aluminum foil lightly over the dish, and bake for about 50 minutes, turning the dish once.
7. Take the foil off, and continue baking for 10 - 15 minutes, until the internal temperature is 155F.  
8. Remove the rabbit pieces, transfer the liqued to a small saucepan, and reduce a bit.
9. Serve with mashed potatoes (skin on) and a big white wine, such as a white Bordeaux, white Cotes du Rhone blend or a buttery California Chardonnay
