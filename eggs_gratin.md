## Eggs Gratin   
[Jacques Pepin YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AvN7-L873Ew)    

#### Ingredients   
* 3 hard boiled eggs, sliced  
* Ham, chopped up  
* Béchamel, modified (see below)
  * Tablespoon of butter
  * Tablespoon of flour
  * Salt & pepper
  * Cream
  * Milk   
* 1 - 2 mushrooms, chopped
* 1/4 cup of cream
* 1/4 cup of milk   
* 1 cup of mozzarella or Swiss cheese
* Grated parmesan cheese  
* Chives, chopped   

#### Preparation    
1. Hard boil the eggs and slice
2. Chop up mushroom(s)
3. Chop up chives
4. Chop up mozzarella
5. Grate parmesan   
6. Make the Béchamel sauce
7. Preheat oven to 400F   

#### Directions   
1. Put the sliced eggs in the bottom of the gratin dish.
2. Add mozzarella or Swiss cheese on top.
3. Add the sliced ham.
4. Bake at 400F for 20 minutes (?)   
5. And chopped chives.  
6. Pour on the sauce and settle it in.
7. Put under the broiler until it browns.   
 
#### Sauce   
* Melt butter and stir in flour to make a roux (10 - 15 seconds).   
* Add the milk and bring to a boil.   
* Stir in the chopped mushroom(s).    
* Stir in cream.
