## Braised Leeks   

#### Ingredients    
* Leeks, cleaned, trimmed, and cut into 1 - 2 in rounds
* 1/2 cup of butter
* 1/3 cup of dry white wine
* 1/2 cup of cream
* 1/2 cup of grated parmesan cheese
* Flaky sea salt
* A pinch of fresh rosemary leaves, finely chopped
* Bay leaves
* Fresh tarragon leaves   


#### Preparation   
1. Clean and cut the leeks
2. Grate the parmesan
3. Chop rosemary leaves   
4. Preheat oven to 350F   

#### Directions   
1. Lightly butter the bottom of a baking dish and sprinkle with flaky sea salt.    
2. Arrange the leeks snugly (standing up) and sprinkle with more salt.    
3. Melt the butter in a small pot set over medium low heat.    
4. Add the rosemary and let it gently sizzle for about a minute.
5. Pour in the white wine and creamer, then season with salt and freshly cracked pepper.
6. Stir to combine.
7. Pour the mixture over the leeks.
8. Tuck the bay leaves between the leeks.
9. Roast for 45 - 60 minutes, until the leeks are tender. [See note, below]   
10. About 10 minutes before done, sprinkle on parmesan and tarragon & return to oven to brown.  

Note: For silkier, softer leeks, cover with aluminum foil
