 ## Poulet Chasseur   

 #### Ingredients   
* Chicken legs    
* Shallot    
* Garlic    
* Mushrooms   
* White wine    
* 1/2 a can of peeled or chopped tomatoes    
* Olive oil
* Soya sauce   
* Potato or corn starch   
* Fresh thyme
* Fresh tarragon   

#### Preparation   
1. Separate thighs from drumsticks, loosen the thigh bones, and chop off the ends of the drumsticks
2. Thinly slice the shallot
3. Mince the garlic
4. Slice the mushrooms
5. Chop the tarragon   

#### Directions   
1. Heat some olive oil in a pan, and brown the skin side of the chicken.
2. When the skin is nice a crispy brown, turn it over and continue for a few minutes.
3. Add the sliced shallot and cook for a few minutes.
4. Add the garlic and cook until it releases its aroma.
5. Add the mushrooms and cook until they start to sweat.
6. Add the wine, tomatoes, and soya sauce.   
7. Taste and season with salt and pepper as needed.
8. Add the thyme, cover, and gently simmer for 30 - 40 minutes.
9. Remove the chicken, and thicken the sauce with potato or corn starch.
10. Sprinkle with the fresh tarragon before serving.
