## Pan-roasted Brussels Sprouts   

#### Ingredients   
* Brussels sprouts
* Red chili pepper, sliced   
* Parmagiano cheese, grated   
* Garlic, chopped
* Lemon, grated and juiced   
* Olive oil
* Balsamic vinegar

#### Preparation   
1. Trim and half the sprouts    
2. Chop the garlic
3. Slice the red pepper
4. Grate the cheese
5. Grate and juice the lemon   

#### Directions   
1. Put sprouts face down in cold olive oil in a pan, turn up heat.
2. Cook until the sprouts are caremelized.
3. Turn over and cook a few more minutes.
4. Push sprouts to one side and add the garlic and chili pepper.
5. Shake and stir.
6. Add a bit of balsamic vinegar and stir.
7. Add the lemon juice and stir.
8. Add the lemon zest and stir.
9. Add the grated parmgiano and stir.
