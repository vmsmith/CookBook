## *Poulet Ã  la Claudine*   
* Jacques Pepin   

#### Ingredients   
* 2 Chicken thighs, prepped    
* 1/2 cup rice    
* 1/2 cup canned chick peas   
* 1/2 cup of cut onions    
* 1/4 cup of cut scallions    
* 1/2 cup chopped zucchini   
* 3/4 cup hot tomato juice    
* 1/2 cup chicken stock    
* Hot sauce    
* Spanish paprika (or *piment d'Eplette*)    
* Cumin    
* Olive oil
* Salt and pepper


#### Preparation   
1. Prep chicken thighs (cumin, Spanish paprika, olive oil, salt, hot sauce) and let sit overnight   
2. Cup onion
3. Cut scallions
4. Cut zucchini   
5. Preheat oven for 350F    

#### Directions   
1. Put the rice, chick peas, onions, scallion, and zucchini in a baking dish and mix. 
2. Cover baking dish with aluminum foil and place chicken thighs on top.    
3. Cook for an hour.
