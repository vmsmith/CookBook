## Pork Kidneys à la Jacques Pepin   
[YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IpQw_CSGYSo)    

#### Ingredients    
* Pork kidneys
* Onion, finely chopped   
* Garlic, chopped   
* Olive oil
* Butter
* White wine   
* V-8 (or equivalent) juice, spicey if possible      

#### Preparation    
1. Slice the kidney "meat" off the inner fiber
2. Chop onion
3. Chop garlic   

#### Directions   
1. Pat dry & season the pork, then sauté on high heat for 1 - 1 1/2 minutes per side.
2. Remove pork kidneys.
3. Sauté the onions for a few minutes.
4. Add the garlic until the aroma starts.
5. Deglaze the pan with a few tablespoons of white wine.
6. Add a bit of V-8 and reduce.
7. Pour pork liquid into sauce.
8. Serve.
