## Foul Medames   

#### Ingredients   
* 1 cup of dried fava beans    
* Onion    
* Chopped garlic    
* Tomato (fresh or canned)        
* Harissa    
* Egg(s) - Optional   
* Olive oil          
* Cumin    
* Lemon juice   
* Chopped parsley       
* Flat bread     

#### Preparation   
1. Soak the fava beans overnight and peel them
2. Chop the onion
3. Chop the garlic   
4. Chop the tomato    
5. Chop parsley   
 
#### Dirctions   
1. Boil the peeled fava beans in water until they are soft.
2. Sautée the onion in olive oil.
3. Add the garlic, and stir for about 30 seconds.
4. Add the cumin and let it "bloom."
5. Add the chopped tomatoes and harissa and cook for 8-10 minutes, until the tomatoes are "jammy."
6. Season with salt and pepper, and add a few drops of lemon juice.   
7. Optional: Crack the egg(s) into it and cook them.
8. Sprinkle with chopped parsley.
