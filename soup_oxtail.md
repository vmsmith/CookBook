### Oxtail Soup   

#### Ingredients    
* 3 pounds oxtails chopped      
* 6 cups beef broth    
* 1/2 cup red wine or more beef broth    
* 2 small onions chopped
* 2 stalks of celery       
* 2 large carrots chopped        
* 3 cloves garlic minced    
* 1 tablespoon flour    
* 2 tablespoons tomato paste
* 2 tablespoons of Worsechire sauce
* 1/3 cup of dry sherry   
* 2 teaspoons salt    
* 1 teaspoon pepper    
* 1/4 cup oil    
* 2 bay leaves    
* 3 sprigs fresh thyme (1 tsp dried)       
* 1/4 cup fresh parsley    
* 1 star anise    

#### Preparation    
1. Chop the onion
2. Chop the carrots into 1" lengths   
3. Chop the celery
4. Mince the garlic   

#### Directions   
1. Season the oxtails with salt and pepper on all sides, then brown and set aside.    
2. Cook the onion and celery in a pot until tender, then add the garlic for a minute or two.   
3. Add flour to the pot and cook until it becomes golden brown, then add the tomato paste and Worceshire sauce and quickly combine.    
4. Pour in the wine and deglaze the pan.
5. Return the meat. 
6. Add spices and cook for 1-2 minutes.
7. Pour in the beef broth and simmer for 2 1/2 hours, or until the meat is tender.
8. Skim off fat, and add carrots   
9. Once cooked, discard the bones, shred the meat, and serve the soup.
