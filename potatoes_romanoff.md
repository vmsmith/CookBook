## Potatoes Romanoff   

#### Ingredients    
* Baking potato, grated       
* Bacon    
* Bacon bits       
* Shallots, chopped       
* Sour cream    
* White cheddar, shredded       
* Butter    
* Parmigiano-Reggiano, shredded
* Herb for garnish   

#### Preparation    
1. Prepare potatoes one day in advance [see note]
2. Grate the potatoes  
3. Shred cheddar
4. Shred Parmigiano
5. Butter a baking dish   
6. Preheat oven to 425F    

#### Directions    
1. Cook the bacon & shallots.    
3. Put the grated potatoes in a bowl.    
4. Add sour cream and shredded cheddar.    
5. Add a few ounces of butter.    
6. Add the bacon/shallots.    
7. Stir and mix well, then put in baking dish.
8. Sprikle with shredded parmigiano and bacon bits.
9. In the oven for 40 - 45 minutes (inside temp 150+)
10. Sprinkle with chopped chives or parsley. 


#### Note   
1. Brush skin of potato    
2. Wrap in parchiment paper    
3. Wrap in aluminum foil   
4. Poke a few holes in the foil
5. Bake 1t 400F until done (1+ hour)   
6. Peel (and save the skin)
7. Put in fridge **uncovered** all night
8. Grate
