### Country Omelette Ã  la Jacques Pepin  

#### Ingredients  

* Eggs  
* Potatoes  
* Onions  
* Tomatoes  
* Chives  
* Olive oil  
* Butter  

#### Preparation  

1. Break and beat eggs  
2. Peel and slice potatoes  
3. Slice onions  
4. Slice tomatoes  

#### Directions  

1. Cook potato and onion slices  

2. Add egg and continue to cook  

3. Place tomato slices on top  

4. Broil
