### Venison Chili   

#### Ingredients    
* 1 pound of ground venison    
* 1 1/2 cups of venison of beef stock    
* 28 ounces of diced tomatoes     
* 15 ounces of kidney beans, drained and rinsed    
* 15 ounces of black beans, drained and rinsed     
* 1 yellow onion, diced     
* 2 peppers, diced     
* 4 - 5 garlic cloves, minced     
* 2 tbsp chili powder    
* 1 tbsp of cumin
* 1 tsp of paprika
* 1 1/2 tsp salt
* 1/2 tsp pepper
* 3 tbsp tomato paste
* 1 tsp Worchestishire sauce   
* 1 orange, juiced
* 1 lime, juiced    

#### Preparation    
* Dice the onion
* Dice the peppers
* Mince the garlic
* Juice the orange
* Juice the lime
