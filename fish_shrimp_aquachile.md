## Shrimp Aquachile  

#### Ingredients   
* 1 pound medium shrimp, peeled, deveined, and cut in half lengthwise (see Tip)
* 1/4 red onion, cut into small dice
* Fine sea salt
* 2 to 3 serrano chiles, stems and seeds removed, chopped
* 1/2 cup fresh lime juice (from 5 to 6 limes)
* 1 packed cup cilantro leaves and tender stems
* 1 tablespoon avocado oil or other vegetable oil
* 1 ripe Hass avocado
* Crushed red pepper, optional
* 5 tostadas, broken into quarters (or use tortilla chips)
