### Lemon Cream Sauce with Capers and Shallots  
Serve on pasta or fish (salmon)  

#### Ingredients  

* Butter  
* Shallots, chopped  
* Capers  
* Lemon juice (half a lemon)  
* Heavy cream  
* Parsely, chopped  


#### Preparation  

1. Chop the shallots  
2. Chop the parsley  

#### Directions  

1. Melt the butter  
2. Saut√© the shallots and capers in it  
3. Add the lemon juice, and cook it down  
4. Add the cream  
5. Stir in the parsely
