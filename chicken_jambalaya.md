## Chicken Jambalaya   

#### Ingredients   
* Chicken thighs, skin off
* Onions
* Green pepper, chopped
* Red pepper, chopped
* Orange pepper, chopped
* Celery stalk, chopped
* Smoked sausage, chopped
* 2 - 3 tablespoons of tomato purée
* 1 cup of chopped tomatoes
* Fresh thyme
* Fresh tarragon, chopped
* Cayenne pepper
* Several garlic cloves, finely chopped
* Chicken stock
* Long grain rice  

#### Preparation    
1. Slice the onions
2. Deskin and **brown** the chicken
3. Seed and chop green, red, and orange peppers
4. Chop the celery stalk
5. Chop the smoked sausage
6. Chop tarragon
7. Chop the garlic cloves   

#### Directions   
1. Caremelize the onions.    
2. Add the chopped green and red peppers, and stir for a few minutes.   
3. Add the chopped celery.
4. Add the smoked sausage.
5. Add the tomato purée and chopped tomatoes.
6. Add the fresh herbs.
7. Add some cayenne.
8. Add the garlic.
9. Add the stock and rice.
10. Add chicken thighs.
11. Season.
12. Cook over low heat for about an hour.
