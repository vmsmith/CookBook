## Chicken Marbella    

#### Ingredients    
* 4 chicken legs, prepped
* 1 cup of white wine   
* 1/2 cup olive oil
* 1/2 cup red wine vinegar
* 1 1/2 cups of prunes   
* 1 cup of pitten green olives
* 1/2 cup capers (with juice)
* 6 bay leaves
* 1 1/2 heads of minced garlic
* 1/4 cup of dried oregano
* 2 tablespoons of salt
* 2 teaspoons of pepper   

#### Preparation    
* Lop off ends of drumsticks    
* Mix olive oil, red wine vinegar, prunes, olives, capers (with juice), bay leaves, garlic, salt, pepper
* Add chicken, mix well, put in fridge overnight
