## Chicken Marbella    

#### Ingredients    
* 4 chicken legs, prepped
* 1 cup of white wine   
* 1/2 cup olive oil
* 1/2 cup red wine vinegar
* 3/4 cups of prunes
* 3/4 cups of dried apricots      
* 1 cup of pitten green olives
* 1/2 cup capers (with juice)
* 6 bay leaves
* 1 1/2 heads of minced garlic
* 1/4 cup of dried oregano
* 2 tablespoons of salt
* 2 teaspoons of pepper
* Brown sugar   

#### Preparation    
1. Lop off ends of drumsticks
2. Mix olive oil, red wine vinegar, prunes, olives, capers (with juice), bay leaves, garlic, salt, pepper
3. Add chicken, mix well, put in fridge overnight   

#### Directions    
1. Take mixture out of fridge, put in baking pan, expose chicken skin side up.
2. Sprinkle a little bit of brown sugar on top.
3. Salt and pepper.
4. Roast at 350F for 50 minutes (internal temp: 145 F).   
5. Cover pan tightly with aluminum foil, and sit for 15 minutes.
