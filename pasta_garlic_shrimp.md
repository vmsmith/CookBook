## Garlic Shrimp Pasta   

#### Ingredients    
* Spaghetti (or linguine)    
* Fresh shrimp, peeled and deveined        
* Garlic cloves    
* Shallot   
* Fresno pepper    
* Olive oil    
* Lemon    
* Dry white wine    
* Unsalted butter    
* Grated Parmigiano Reggiano or Pecorino Romano     
* Parsley     
* Salt and pepper       

#### Preparation   
1. Peel and devein the shrimp
2. Chop the garlic
3. Chop the shallot
4. Chop the Fresno pepper
5. Chop the parsley   
6. Grate the cheese   


#### Directions   
1. Start cooking the pasta.
2. Season the shrimp with salt and pepper, then cook in olive oil for 90 seconds per side, then remove.    
3. Add a bit more olive oil to the pan.
4. Sautée the garlic, shallots, and Fresno pepper (pinch of salt).
5. Add a squeeze of lemon juice, then deglaze the pan with white wine and reduce the wine.
6. Add some of the pasta cooking liquid and some unsalted butter & stir.
7. Once the butter is melted, strain the pasta and add it.
8. Fold in the shrimp and chopped parsley.
