## Chicken Thighs in Creamy Tarragon Sauce   
* Jacques Pepin   

#### Ingredients    
* Chicken thighs
* Onion, chopped
* 1 tablespoon of flour
* 1/4 cup of white wine
* 3/4 cup of chicken stock
* 1/4 cup of cream
* Fresh tarragon, chopped    

#### Preparation    
1. Remove skin from chicken thights & detach bones   
2. Chop onion
3. Chop tarragon   

#### Directions   
1. Brown chicken thighs in pan with melted butter (about 4 minutes).    
2. Add half a cup of onions & cook for a few more minutes.
3. Add a tablespoon of flour and stir.
4. Add the wine.
5. Add the stock.
6. Cook for about 30 minutes.
7. Add the cream.
8. Add **most of** the chopped tarragon.
9. Serve with rice.
