## Ramen Soup with Shrimp and Vegetables
* Jacques Pepin on FaceBook

#### Ingredients   
* 2 1/2 cups of chicken stock
* Ramen noodles
* Raw, peeled, and deveined hrimp, chopped   
* 1 - 2 hard boiled eggs, cut in half      
* 1/2 cup of onion, chopped   
* 1 carrot, cut into cubes  
* 1 cup of shittake mushrooms, sliced   
* Garlic, chopped
* Bok choi, chopped up     
* 1 1/2 teaspoons of grated ginger
* 1 teaspoon of chili garlic sauce
* 1 tablespoon of fish sauce

#### Preparation   
1. Chop all the vegetables
2. Peel, devein, and chop the shrimp  
3. Cut the egg(s) in half
4. Boil the ramen
5. Heat the chicken stock    

#### Directions   
1. Add the chopped onion, carrot, mushrooms, garlic, bok choi, garlic, and ginger to the hot chicked stock.
2. Let cook for about five minutes.
3. Add the shrimp.
4. Add the cooked ramen noodles.
5. Granish with the halved hard boiled eggs.
