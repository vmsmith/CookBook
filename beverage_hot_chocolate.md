### Hot Chocolate  

#### Ingredients  

* 1/2 cup hot chocolate (Perignotti or De Zaan)  
* 1/3 cup sugar  
* 1+ teaspoon corn starch  
* 1 cup water  
* 1 cup milk 

#### Directions  

1. Over a low flame, whisk together:

* Cocoa powder
* Cornstarch
* Sugar
* 1/2 cup of water.

2. Once dissolved, add

* 1/2 cup of water
* 1 cup of milk

3. Stir until it thickens
