## Chicken Scallopini with Mushrooms and Zucchini    

#### Ingredients   
* Chicken breast cut (and pounded)   
* Zucchini   
* Muchrooms   
* Cherry tomatoes   
* Scallions   
* Garlic   
* Olive oil   
* Butter   
* Tarragon   
* Salt, pepper   
* Paprika or piment d'Eplette   

#### Preparation    
1. Season the chicken breast with salt, pepper, and heat.      
2. Coarsely chop the mushroom.   
3. Julienne the zucchini.    
4. Chop the scallions.  
5. Slice the cherry tomatoes.  

#### Directions   
1. Sauté the mushrooms in olive oil until the water is gone.   
2. Add the zucchini and cook for a few minutes.   
3. Remove the mushrooms and zucchini and keep warm.  
4. Add a touch more olive oil to the pan.    
5. Cook the chicken for 1 - 1 1/2 minutes per side, then remove and lay over vegetables.  
6. Add a bit of butter, and the garnish.   
7. Add a splash of water and cook down.   
8. Lay garnish over the chicken.
