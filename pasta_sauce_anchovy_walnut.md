## Anchovy and Walnut Pasta Sauce   

#### Ingredients    
* Linguine or spaghetti    
* Walnuts, broken up        
* Anchovies, cut up    
* Garlic, minced      
* Parmesan cheese, grated    
* Chili flakes
* Fresh parsley, chopped     
* Salt and pepper   

#### Preparation   
1. Cut the anchovies
2. Break up the walnuts   
3. Mince the garlic
4. Grate the cheese
5. Chop some parsley  

#### Directions   
1. Start boiling some pasta.
2. In a dry pan, lightly toast the walnuts, then remove.
3. Add some olive oil, and cook the cut anchovies over medium heat while smooshing them.
4. Stir in some chili flakes.  
5. Add the garlic, and cook it until it starts releasing its fragarance.
6. Add 1/4 cup of the pasta water.
7. Add the *cooked* pasta and stir it all up.
8. Add more water if needed.
9. Add the parmesan cheese and parsley, give it a few tosses, and serve.
