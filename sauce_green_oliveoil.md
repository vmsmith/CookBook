### Green Sauce  
* Alice Waters "Simple Foods"  

#### Ingredients  

* 1/2 cup of good olive oil  
* 1/3 cup of chopped parsley  
* 1 tablespoon of chopped capers 
* Finely chopped garlic cloves  
* Zest of one lemon  
* Salt to taste  

#### Preparation  

* Chop the parsley  
* Chop the garlic  
* Chop the capers  
* Zest the lemon  

#### Directions  

1. Mix everything.
2. Let sit for a while to marry the flavors.
