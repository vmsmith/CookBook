### Lamb *Hachis Parmentier*  

#### Ingredients  
* 1 pound of ground lamb   
* 140 grams of tomato paste   
* 1 onion, chopped   
* Chopped garlic  
* Salt  
* Pepper  
* 2 bay leaves  
* Herbes de Provence   
* Mashed potatoes  
* Handful of grated cheese  

#### Preparation  

1. Make the mashed potatoes with ample cream and butter  
2. Chop the onion  
3. Chop the garlic  
4. Grate cheese
